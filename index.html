The Mastery365 Protocol

Mastery is the mathematical result of unbroken consistency. This tool is built to visualize your Discipline Density. Watch your heatmap turn green and your rank climb.

👤 Identity & Rank

Create your profile. Use the Camera or Upload buttons to cement your dashboard identity. As you log total minutes, your rank badge will evolve from Initiate to Grandmaster.

🏗️ Architecture

Define absolute targets (e.g., "Reading" for 45 mins). You must hit or exceed this target to earn a daily win.

⏱️ Execution & Live Timers

Use the Start timer for deep focus—it now ticks live. Or, manually type your minutes. Hit the target to trigger the dopamine hit and auditory chime.

🏆 Perfect Days & Heatmap

Hit all targets in a day to unlock the "Perfect Day" glow. Consistency unlocks streak multipliers, and your entire year is mapped in the Audit tab.

💾 Backup & Security

Your data lives safely in your browser. Use the Export Save button at the bottom to download a backup file of your progress. Use Import Save to restore it anytime.
